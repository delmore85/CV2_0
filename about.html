A Little About Me

Despite the challenges I face growing up, I lived a normal life and accomplish many milestones. I graduated from high school in 2004, continued onto community college at JCC, then later transferred to Strayer University. For almost ten years I have been with the woman I eventually came to marry. I have consistently been able to find employment by applying myself to a wide range of tasks from folding clothes, to plumbing, to running emergency break fix on servers in a 24hr a day on call.

Since my time in middle school I have always had an interest in IT. Throughout high school, I took as many classes relating to the field as I could, eventually joining STLP (Student Technology Leadership Program). Towards the end of my high school career, I began to see that PC repair and PC building were lacking the challenge I was looking for. Seeking these new challenges I discovered the world of web development and programming. This seemed like the kind of field which would constantly offer growth in learning and the challenges I desired.
